HOW TO PLAY
GOAL

Crack a combination with 3 or more blocks of the same color.

Rember to stay away from the borders.

Press right and left to rotate the Hexagon.

MOVEMENT

Tap left and right to rotate the Hexagon.

Press down to speed up the block falling.
COMBO TIMER

Indicated by the colored lines on the border.

Get to Impressive Pacing and you'll crack all the combinations at once.
